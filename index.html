Who We Are

Tacloban's
Maxim Riders.

AutoMM is a proud community of Maxim delivery riders based in Tacloban City, Leyte. We started as a small circle of riders who believed that staying connected makes us stronger — and today, we ride as one unified brotherhood under the Maxim platform.

  • We are an exclusive community for Maxim riders in Tacloban City — connecting every rider under one brotherhood.
  • We promote road safety, mutual support, and professional delivery standards for all our Maxim riders in Tacloban.
  • We organize group rides, outings, and events around Tacloban and Leyte that keep the brotherhood spirit alive.
  • Our mission: Eat. Sleep. Ride. Repeat. — and never ride the streets of Tacloban alone.
